Project proposal

This is the proposals for a better transportation quality

Infrastructure Upgrades

  • Expansion: Increase the coverage of public transport networks, focusing on underserved areas.
  • Dedicated Lanes: Establish exclusive lanes for buses and other public transport modes to reduce congestion and travel time.

Technological Integrations

  • Real-Time Tracking: Implement GPS tracking systems to provide real-time information on vehicle locations, schedules, and delays to passengers through mobile apps and information boards.
  • Cashless Payments: Enable contactless payment options, such as smart cards or mobile payments, to streamline the boarding process and reduce transaction times.

Regulatory Reforms and Policies

  • Fare Integration: Introduce fare integration across different transport modes to encourage more usage and simplify payment processes for passengers.
  • Regulate Safety Standards: Enforce strict safety regulations for vehicles and infrastructure to ensure passenger safety.

Environmental Considerations

  • Promote Sustainable Modes: Encourage the use of eco-friendly vehicles (electric or hybrid) to reduce emissions and combat pollution (a big deal in Mexico City).

Community Engagement

  • Public Awareness Campaigns: Educate the public about the benefits of using public transport and promote a cultural shift towards its adoption.
  • Feedback Mechanisms: Establish channels for feedback and suggestions from passengers to continuously improve services based on their needs.

Investment and Funding

  • Public-Private Partnerships: Collaborate with private sectors to invest in and improve public transport infrastructure and services.
  • Government Funding: Allocate sufficient funds towards the development and maintenance of public transport systems as a crucial public service.

Accessibility and Inclusivity

  • Accessibility Features: Ensure transport facilities are accessible to people with disabilities, including ramps, designated spaces, and support systems.
